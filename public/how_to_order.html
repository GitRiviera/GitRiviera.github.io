How to order

To order products from LaserBio Labs please consult our catalog and email us a Purchase Order from your Company or Institution. We also accept email orders from repeat customers. Orders by fax or postal service are also accepted.

To request a quote or additional administrative information please contact us by email with your product requirements.

We do not request pre-payment of orders. An invoice is sent electronically at the time of shipment, including bank account information.

All prices are quoted and invoiced exclusively in EUROS. Payment must be made in EUROS by direct bank transfer.

All transfer-related bank fees should be borne by the paying organization. LaserBio Labs will not accept the deduction of bank fees from the amount invoiced. In such case, another invoice including additional bank fees may be emitted or the bank fees may be added to the customer's next order.
